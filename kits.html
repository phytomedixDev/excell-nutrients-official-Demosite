NMN (Nicotinamide Mononucleotide)

500mg▼
NMN is a direct precursor to NAD+, a coenzyme essential for over 500 enzymatic reactions including energy metabolism, DNA repair, and gene expression regulation. NAD+ levels decline approximately 50% between ages 40–60. Oral NMN supplementation has been shown to raise blood NAD+ levels within 2–4 weeks in human clinical trials.
📚 Evidence: Yoshino et al. (2021, Cell Metabolism) — NMN supplementation improved muscle insulin sensitivity and increased NAD+ metabolism in premenopausal women with prediabetes.

Trans-Resveratrol

250mg▼
Trans-resveratrol activates SIRT1 and SIRT3 sirtuins — NAD+-dependent deacetylases that regulate cellular stress responses, mitochondrial biogenesis, and longevity pathways. Functions synergistically with NMN; the two compounds have been termed a "longevity combination" in the research literature.
📚 Evidence: Timmers et al. (2011, Cell Metabolism) — Resveratrol supplementation mimicked caloric restriction effects and improved metabolic health in obese men.

CoQ10 Ubiquinol

200mg▼
Ubiquinol (reduced form of CoQ10) is the primary mitochondrial electron carrier and a potent lipid-soluble antioxidant. Plasma CoQ10 declines with age and is depleted by statin use. At 200mg, the ubiquinol form is absorbed 3–8x more efficiently than ubiquinone, with measurable plasma elevations within 2 weeks.
📚 Evidence: Langsjoen & Langsjoen (2014, Biofactors) — Ubiquinol supplementation at 200mg significantly improved heart failure outcomes vs ubiquinone at equivalent doses.

Vitamin D3 + K2 MK-7

5000IU + 100mcg▼
Vitamin D3 functions as a steroid hormone regulating over 1000 genes, immune function, calcium absorption, and inflammation. K2 MK-7 activates matrix Gla-protein (MGP) which inhibits arterial calcification, directing calcium to bone instead of blood vessels. These compounds work synergistically — D3 increases calcium absorption, K2 determines where it is deposited. Vitamin D deficiency affects an estimated 80%+ of GCC populations due to limited sun exposure.
📚 Evidence: Vermeer et al. (2009, Thrombosis and Haemostasis) — K2 MK-7 supplementation significantly reduced arterial stiffness over 3 years in postmenopausal women.

Radiance & Brightening Protocol

A comprehensive inside-out skin protocol combining oral glutathione, liposomal vitamin C, marine collagen peptides, and targeted topical support — clinically formulated for melanin-rich skin.

  • ✨Melanin inhibition from within — Glutathione shifts melanin synthesis from eumelanin (dark) to phaeomelanin (lighter pigment) through tyrosinase enzyme inhibition.
  • 🔬Collagen synthesis stimulation — Vitamin C is the essential cofactor for collagen hydroxylation. Marine collagen peptides provide the amino acid building blocks (glycine, proline, hydroxyproline).

Week 6–8Significant brightening effect. Reduction in dark spots and uneven pigmentation. Skin luminosity measurably improved. Fine lines less prominent.

Skin brightening from within is significantly more effective and sustainable than topical-only approaches. The oral route allows systemic antioxidant loading that topical products cannot achieve.

L-Glutathione (Reduced)

500mg▼
Glutathione is the body's master antioxidant and primary melanin regulator. At the pigmentation level, it inhibits tyrosinase — the rate-limiting enzyme in melanin synthesis — while simultaneously shifting production from darker eumelanin to lighter phaeomelanin. Critically, the reduced (L-) form is active and maintains its integrity in capsule form when protected from heat and light.
📚 Evidence: Sonthalia et al. (2016, Journal of Cosmetic Dermatology) — Oral glutathione 500mg for 4 weeks produced significant skin brightening with no adverse effects in a randomised controlled trial.

Liposomal Vitamin C

1000mg▼
Vitamin C (ascorbic acid) inhibits melanin synthesis independently of glutathione by reducing dopaquinone back to DOPA, interrupting the melanin cascade. More importantly, it is the essential cofactor for collagen hydroxylation — without adequate vitamin C, pro-collagen chains cannot be properly cross-linked. The liposomal form achieves 3–4x higher plasma levels than standard ascorbic acid tablets.
📚 Evidence: Gęgotek et al. (2021) — Vitamin C supplementation significantly increased skin collagen density and reduced UV-induced oxidative damage markers.

Berberine HCl

500mg × 3▼

📚 Evidence: Yin et al. (2008, Metabolism) — Berberine 500mg × 3 daily reduced HbA1c by 2.0% and fasting glucose by 25.9% in T2D patients over 3 months.
