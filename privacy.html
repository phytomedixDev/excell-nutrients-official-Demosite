Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Excell Nutrients is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with UAE Federal Law No. 45 of 2021 on Personal Data Protection.

  • How We Use It

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Name, email address, phone number when creating an account or contacting us
  • Billing and shipping address for order fulfilment
  • Health and biometric data entered in our Digital Twin Assessment
  • Communications you send us via email, WhatsApp, or our contact form

Information collected automatically

  • Device information, browser type, IP address, and geographic location
  • Pages visited, time on site, and interaction patterns via Google Analytics
  • Purchase history and product preferences
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Cookies section)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to fulfil orders and deliver products; provide customer support; generate your personalised Digital Twin supplement protocol; send transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates); improve our products and services; comply with UAE law and regulatory requirements; and send marketing communications (only with your explicit consent).

We process your data based on: contract performance (to fulfil your order), legitimate interests (improving our services), legal obligation, and consent (marketing communications).

3. Health Data

Information you provide in our Digital Twin Assessment — including health conditions, medications, biometric measurements, and lifestyle factors — is treated as sensitive personal data. We apply the following protections:

  • Health data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access is restricted to authorised clinical and technical personnel only
  • Health data is used solely to generate your supplement protocol and is not sold to third parties
  • Our Digital Twin engine is powered by Phytomedix technology — data processing is governed by their data processing agreement
  • You may request deletion of all health data at any time

By completing the Digital Twin Assessment, you provide explicit consent for the processing of your health data for the purpose of generating personalised supplement recommendations.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Phytomedix: Our licensed Digital Twin technology provider, for protocol generation
  • Payment processors: Stripe or local payment gateways for secure transaction processing
  • Shipping partners: Courier services for order delivery
  • Email service providers: For transactional and marketing communications
  • Analytics providers: Google Analytics (anonymised data only)

All third parties are contractually bound to process your data only for specified purposes and in compliance with applicable data protection law.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to: maintain your login session; remember your cart and preferences; analyse website traffic via Google Analytics; and enable payment processing functionality.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

Under UAE Federal Law No. 45 of 2021, you have the right to: access your personal data; correct inaccurate data; request deletion of your data; object to certain processing; data portability; and withdraw consent at any time (for consent-based processing).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@excellnutrients.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data for: account data — as long as your account is active; order data — 7 years (UAE tax law requirement); health assessment data — 2 years after last login unless you request earlier deletion; marketing consent records — until consent is withdrawn plus 3 years.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit, AES-256 encryption for health data at rest, regular security audits, multi-factor authentication for staff accounts, and access controls limiting data access to authorised personnel.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@excellnutrients.com
  • Phone: +971 50 917 2876
  • Address: Dubai, United Arab Emirates

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the UAE Data Office if you believe your data has been processed unlawfully.
