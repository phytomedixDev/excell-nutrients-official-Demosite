FAQ

Common questions

How long does delivery take in the UAE?+
Orders placed before 2pm UAE time are dispatched same day. Standard delivery is 1-2 business days within Dubai and Abu Dhabi, 2-3 days to other emirates. Free shipping on orders over AED 200.
Can I take supplements with my prescription medications?+
This is exactly what our Digital Twin assessment checks. It cross-references your medications against all recommended supplements and flags any interactions. For complex cases, we route you to a clinical review. Always consult your physician for specific advice.
Are your products Halal certified?+
Yes, 100% of our products are Halal certified by UAE Islamic Affairs. No porcine derivatives, no alcohol-based ingredients, no animal-based capsules. We use only plant-based capsules across our entire range.
What is the Digital Twin assessment?+
It's a 10-minute clinically-structured assessment of your biometrics, health conditions, lifestyle, and symptoms. The AI engine evaluates 500+ biological variables to produce a personalised supplement protocol. It's free, and you can start at twin.excellnutrients.com.
Do you ship to Saudi Arabia and East Africa?+
Yes. We ship to UAE, KSA, Kenya, Tanzania, and Rwanda. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Contact our regional offices for specific shipping quotes.
What is your return policy?+
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all products. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact us for a full refund or exchange. Opened products are eligible for return within 14 days if you are unsatisfied with results.
